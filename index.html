Shrinidhi Rao

Shrin is someone who believes most of the big problems are solvable, that most people want to help, and that the gap between those two facts is mostly logistical. This site is his attempt to close that gap — a little bit, in a few places, for a few people.

He's drawn to the hard, human work of building communities that hold each other up: connecting people to resources they didn't know existed, making it easier to show up for neighbors, and finding ways to live a little more gently on the planet. Economic justice, civic life, and mutual aid aren't abstractions to him — they're the texture of a life worth living.

This is a personal project, not a professional one. The resources, events, and experiments here reflect what he actually cares about — not a resume, not a brand.
